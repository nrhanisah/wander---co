11 Days Aurora

From RM 25,288 PER PAX

Day 1: Penang - Transit City
Assemble at Airport for flight to Oslo (The capital of Norway).

Day 2: Transit City - Oslo (Norway)
Upon arrival, proceed to city orientation tour including City Hall & Vigeland Park. Next, visit Karl Johan Street.

Day 3: Oslo - Kirkenes (Norway)
Transfer to domestic airport for flight to Kirkenes. Upon arrival, tourist may experience to catch King Crab and enjoy Artic King Crab meal.

Day 4: Kirkenes - Inari (Findland) - Saariselka (Finland)
Proceed to Inari. Upon arrival visit to Siida Sami Museum. Then, continue to Saariselka.

Day 5: Saariselka - Luosto - Rovaniemi (Finland)
Tour transfer to Rovaniemi via Luosto. Then, visit to Amethyst Mine. Board the Pendolino up to te Snow mountain top wuth a fabulous view and tourist may enjoy the Aurora experience.

Day 6: Rovaniemi - Kemi
Visit Santa Claus Village and meet Santa Claus by crossing the magical Artic Circle. Visit Reindeer farm and experience Reindeer Ride.

Day 7: Kemi - Ranua - Kemi
Visit Ranua Zoo. Next, tourist may experience the Husky Dog Ride.

Day 8: Kemi - Lulea (Sweden)
Experience Ice Breaker cruise including ice floating event.

Day 9: Lulea - Stockholm
Transfer to airport for flight to Stockholm. Upon arrival, city orientation include City Hall.

Day 10: Stockholm - Transit City
Tourist are free by their own leisure before transfer to airport for flight to Penang.

Day 11: Transit City - Penang
Arrival Penang.

10 Days Greece

From RM 13,688 PER PAX

Day 1: Penang - Transit City
Assemble at Penang International Airport for flight to Capital of Greece, Athens.

Day 2: Transit City - Chitose - Athens
Athens City orientation tour such as Syntagma Square and stop at the Panathenaic and Stadium. Next, visit World Famous site of Acropolis, the Parthenon.

Day 3: Athens - Diakopto Kalavrita
Visit the Olive Farm. Proceed to take train from Diakopto to Kalavrita.

Day 4: Kalavrita - Corinth Canal Cruise - Athens
Enjoy Corinth Canal Cruise. Then, proceed to Nafplio before back to Athens.

Day 5: Athens - Mykonos
Transfer to the port of Rafina and depart by super ferry to Mykonos (one of the famous Greek Island). Then walk around the town enjoying the shops and boutiques.

Day 6: Mykonos - Santorini
Proceed to Santorini by ferry.

Day 7: Santorini
Walking tour including Fira Town, traditional village of Oia. Later walk on the beautiful volcanic Black Sand Beach of Kamari.

Day 8: Santorini - Athens
Transfer to take super ferry to Athens. Upon arrival, transfer to hotel relax and enjoy.

Day 9: Athens - Transit City
Proceed to Athens city orientation tour. Next, transfer to International Airport for flight to Penang.

Day 10: Arrival Penang
Arrival Penang.
