THAILAND

5 Days 4 Night Bangkok + Kanchanaburi

From RM 2,788 PER PAX

Day 1: Penang - Bangkok
Penang International Airport for departure flight to Bangkok. Upon arrival, transfer to Jatujak Plaza and Jodd Fair DanNeramit enjoy shopping.

Day 2: Bangkok - Kachanaburi
After breakfast, proceed to Kanchanaburi. Enroute we will visit Moai Coffee Suan Pheng and Baan Hom Tien Candle-making shop cafe. Next to visit The Scenery Vintage Farm and Wat Mettadhamma Pothiyan. Upon arrival to Kanchanaburi , will having Local Mookata + Seafood Buffet.

Day 3: Kachanaburi
After breakfast, proceed to enjoy classic train to station Tham Krasae. Next visit the Bridge Over The River Kwai , Malika R.E 124 Village and Chan Nature Cafe. After that shopping at JJ local night market.

Day 4: Kanchanaburi - Bangkok
After breakfast, tour visit to Sky Walk Kanchanaburi, Wat Tham Sua (Include cable car) and Gaint Rain Tree. After that transfer back to Bangkok, visit to Four Face Buddha and Honey Factory. Continue shopping at Hua Mun Night Market.

Day 5: Bangkok
Transfer to airport for flight back to Penang.

5 Days Chiang Mai & Chiang Rai

From RM 2,888 PER PAX

Day 1: Penang - Transit City - Chiang Mai
Penang International Airport for departure flight to Chiangmai. Upon arrival, city orientation of Chiang Mai City Walls and 3 Kings Monument. Next visit to Chedi Luang Temple. At night, enjoy Chiangmai Traditional Khantoke Dinner with show.

Day 2: Chiang Mai
Proceed to visit Wat Baan Den (Blue Temple), Elephant Camp to enjoy Elephant Show + Elephant Ride, Long Neck Village, Doi Suthep Temple and Royal Flora Garden.

Day 3: Chiang Mai - Chiang Rai
Proceed to Chiangrai, upon arrival, visit to Mae Sai, Golden Triangle + Boat trip along Mae Khong River, visit House Of Opium.

Day 4: Chiang Rai - Chiang Mai
Visit Wat Rong Khun (White Temple), Wat Rong Sue Ten (Blue Temple). After proceed back to Chiang Mai on the way short stop at Mae Kajan Hot Spring. Visit Bor Sang Handicraft. At night, free shopping at Chiangmai Night Market.

Day 5: Chiang Mai - Transit - Penang
Transfer to airport for flight back to Penang.
