Itinerary

Kundasang 4 Days 3 Nights RM1,450 per pax

  • Day 3: Kota Kinabalu - Kundasang

    • Head to Kundasang at 7am
    • Mesilou Highland Strawberry Farm
    • Kinabalu Park

  • Day 4:Kundasang

    • Poring Hot Spring
