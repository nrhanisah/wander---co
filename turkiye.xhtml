TÜRKIYE

6 Days 5 Night Iconic of Turkey

From RM 7,210 PER PAX

Day 1: Arrive Istanbul - Bursa
Upon arrival at Istanbul International Airport and meet with the tour guide. Drive to Bursa and transfer to hotel for dinner and overnight.

Day 2: Bursa -Cappadocia
After breakfast at the hotel and check-out, visit Green Mosque & Tomb, Grand Mosque and Silk Market. Stop over at Turkish Delight Shop after lunch. Depart for Cappadocia, transfer to the hotel for dinner and overnight.

Day 3: Cappadocia
Optional Hot Air Balloon Breakfast at the hotel and today have a full day Cappadocia tour. Visit Goreme Open Air Museum, Pigeon Valley, Devrent Valley, Uchisar Towen, Red Valley, Avanos Town and Love Valley. Continue to visit Jewellery Store, Carpet Fabric and Pottery Fabric as well. Dinner and overnight in Cappadocia.

Day 4: Cappadocia - Istanbul
After breakfast at the hotel and check out, depart for Istanbul. On the way, visit Salt Lake, Caravansarai and Underground City. Upon arrival in Istanbul, transfer to the hotel for dinner and overnight.

Day 5: Istanbul
Breakfast at the hotel, proceed to Live Fashion Show. Then visit Roman Hippodrome, Blue Mosque, Topkapı Palace(Photo Stop on 1st Courtyard), St Sophia Mosque (Outside View) and Grand Bazaar. Dinner and overnight in Istanbul.

Day 6: Istanbul - Departure
After breakfast, check-out from the hotel. Drop to shopping mall if time permit. Free at your own leisure till transfer to the airport for your flight departure.

  • ✔ 5N accommodation
  • ✔ Tour and meals as per itinerary
  • ✔ Air-conditioned minibus/coach for transfer and sightseeing
  • ✔ Local English speaking guide

5 Days 4 Night Highlights of Istanbul

From 3,180 PER PAX

Day 1: Arrive Istanbul
Upon arrival at Istanbul International Airport and meet with the tour guide. Transfer to hotel for dinner and overnight.

Day 2: Istanbul
Breakfast at the hotel, proceed for Istanbul city tour. Visit Roman Hippodrome, Blue Mosque, Topkapı Palace (Photo Stop on 1st Courtyard), St Sophia Mosque (Outside View), Spice Market. Dinner and overnight at Istanbul.

Day 3: Istanbul
After breakfast at the hotel, visit the Leather Outlet Center. Following free time for shopping. Continue to Ortakoy Town where famous for Ortakoy Kumpir or Waffle, Taksim Square and lunch will be arranged as Wrapped Doner Kebab. After that, walk throughout Istiklal Street till Galata Area. Next, walk to Karakoy and Galata Bridge. Taste Fish Sandwich. Finish day tour and back to hotel. Dinner and overnight at Istanbul.

Day 4: Istanbul
After breakfast at the hotel, drive to Eminonu Pier and take public boat to Uskudar Town. After disembarking, visit walk to Maiden Tower Area then free time tea & coffee. Continue drive to Kadikoy Town. Kadikoy is most popular town of Asian Side. We will have free time at there to taste street food, have seat at coffee houses and pubs, taste fresh fruits, see wall paintings. Take public boat to cross European Side. Dinner and overnight at Istanbul.

Day 5: Istanbul - Departure
After breakfast, check-out from the hotel. Drop to shopping mall if time permit. Free at your own leisure till transfer to the airport for your flight departure.
