INDONESIA

4 Days Medan

From RM 1,598 PER PAX

Day 1: Penang - Medan - Parapat
Departure flight from Penang International Airport to Medan. Upon arrival, proceed to Lake Toba (Indonesia largest lake).

Day 2: Parapat - Samosir Island - Parapat - Berastagi
Visit Tomok Village, Old Tomb of Raja Sidabutar, Batak Toba, Ambarita Villgae and Sipiso-piso Waterfall.

Day 3: Berastagi - Medan
Fruit Market, Taman Alam Lumbini (Replica of "Shwedagon Pagoda" in Yangon, Myanmar covered in gold), Maimoon Palace, Cemara Asli Temple and Sun Plaza (biggest shopping centre in Medan).

Day 4: Medan - Penang
Transfer to airport for departure flight home.

5 Days Bali

From RM 3,588 PER PAX

Day 1: Penang - Transit -Bali
Departure flight from Penang International Airport. Overnight at Fairfield South Kuda.

Day 2: Bali
Fast Boat Ride to Toya Pakeh Harboh on Penida Island. Next, visit Southern coast for the scenic ocean view of the Paluang and Kelingking cliff.

Day 3: Bali
Vintage Volkswagen Open Top Jeep Trails (Car Ride). Baha Village Mr.Ida Bagus Private House ( house built traditionally in Balinese architecture and design). Then, view rice field and learn the traditional Balinese Irrigation system at the Jatiluwih Green (cultural tradition heritage of Bali), Tamblingan Lake (Part of UNESCO-listed Bali Barat National Park) and Tanah Lot (one of the most important seaside temples in Bali, built in the 16th century.

Day 4: Bali
Balinese traditional village of Penglipuran. View Active volcano of Mount Batur, PicHeaven Swing Bali.

Transfer to ariport for departure flight to Penang.
