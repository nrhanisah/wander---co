TIPS

📋

DOCUMENTATION

1. Check passport validity (at least 6 months before expiry)

2. Make digital copies of important documents

3. Keep digital scans of your visas, corporate invite letters, and insurance policies secure in a cloud drive accessible offline.

🏥

HEALTH & SAFETY

1. Keep emergency contact numbers handy.

2. Aware of local customes and laws.

3. Avoid displaying expensive items in public.

🧳

PACKING TIPS

1. Pack light and bring versatile clothing.

2. Use packing cubes to save space.

3. Carry essential medications in carry-on bag.

🎰

MONEY-SAVING TIPS

1. Travel during off-peak seasons.

2. Set a daily travel budget.

3.Exchange currency before arriving at the destination.

FAQ

How do I book a trip?

You can book online, contact us via WhatsApp or walk in to our office.

How far in advance should I book?

For high-profile executive retreats and international corporate groups, we recommend bookings at least 3 to 6 months in advance.

What payments method accepted?

We accept bank transfer, credit/debit card and e-wallet. No cash.

Can I change travel dates after booking?

Date changes may be possible, subject to availability and additional charges.

Are flights included in the package?

Some packages include flights, depends on the packages choosen.

Is travel insurance included?

Comprehensive global travel insurance coverage packages can be layered into your custom team booking options before final approval.
