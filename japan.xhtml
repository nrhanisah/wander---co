7 Days Hokkaido (Winter)

From RM 9,988 PER PAX

Day 1: Penang - Transit
Departure flight from Penang International Airport to Chitose.

Day 2: Transit - Chitose - Otaru - Lake Toya
Otaru Canal Kita Ichi Glass Shop & Otaru Music Box Museum.

Day 3: Lake Toya -Mt Showa Shinzan - Tokachikawa
Bear Ranch ( Brown bears in zoo with concrete enclosures & vencing machines with food for the animals), Mount ShowaShinzan ( A volcanic lava dome in the Shikotsu-Toya National Park), Noboribetsu Jigokudani (Dramatic crater with boiling sulfuric hot springs, volcanic steam olumes & hiking paths) & Tokachikawa (Hitaka Highland with panoramic view).

Day 4: Tokachikawa - Lake Akan
Kushiro Shitsugen Natinal Park ( To spot Red-crown Crane), Lake Mashu (A caldera lake lying 315 meters above sea level & Mt I-O Live Volcano (Full with sulfure smell's active volcano 510 meters high above sea level).

Day 5: Lake Akan -Abashiri - Sounkyo
Abashiri Ryu-Hyo-Kan Ice Glaciers, Abashiri Prison Museum ( Prison life during the Meiji era) & Ginga and Ryusei Falls (Graceful waterfalls flow like shining silver threads).

Day 6: Sounkyo -Asahikawa - Sapporo
Asahiyama Zoo, Hokkaido Do-Cho (Government Building, one of Japan tallest and largest buildings built in 1888) & Odori Park.

Day 7: Sapporo -Mt Transit - Penang
Transfer to airport for departure flight home.
