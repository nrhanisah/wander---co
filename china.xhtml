8 Days Beijing

From RM 6,288 PER PAX

Day 1: Penang - Transit
Assemble at Penang International Airport for flight to Beijing.

Day 2: Transit - Beijing
National Palace Museum (one of the largest ancient culture and art museums in China), Temple of Heaven (an ancient Chinese royal sacrificial building complex. It was built in the 18th year of the Yongle reign of the Ming Dynasty,1420) and Internet famous attraction Taikoo Li, Sanlitun.

Day 3: Beijing
Great Wall Badaling, Winter Olympics Shougang Big Jumping Platform ("Xue Feitian" Celebrity attraction Wukesong Huaxi LIVE Food Street, there are delicacies from all over the world, all kinds of delicacies, guests can freely choose to eat while shopping -own expense).

Day 4: Beijing - Gubei Water Town
Qianmen Antique Street, the world's largest Starbucks flag waypoint and PAGEONE creative bookstore and Gubei Water Town (including 1 way buggy, Sima Shaojiu Workshop, Yongshun Dyeing Workshop, Sun Moon Square/Zhenyuan Escort Bureau/Eight Banners Guild Hall), and enjoy the night view of the water town.

Day 5: Gubei Water Town - Cheng De
Summer Resort (largest existing royal garden in China) and enjoy the panoramic view of Chengde City and visit Pule Temple.

Day 6: Cheng De - Qinhuangdao
Qinhuangdao ( It is a coastal tourist city with historical culture, seaside scenery and natural ecology). Visit Shanhaiguan, Laolongtou, Qinhuang Alley Food Street.

Day 7: Qinhuangdao - Beijing
Qinhuangdao Commercial Pedestrian Street, where Qinhuangqiu Mountain enters the sea. Labubu Land (located in Chaoyang Park, Chaoyang District, Beijing, is the first immersive IP theme park created by Pop Mart). Outview Bird's Nest and water cube.

Day 8: Beijing - Transit City - Penang
Arrived Penang.

9 Days Kunming + Shangri-La

From RM 7,688 PER PAX

Day 1: Penang - Kunming
Assemble at Penang International Airport and take direct flight to Kunming. Upon arrival, check in to the hotel.

Day 2: Kunming - Dali
After breakfast, take a bus to the east coast of Erhai Lake in Dali to visit the ideal country (China's Santorini ). Then visit Dali Ancient Town (Foreigner Street).

Day 3: Dali- Shangri-La
Proceed to Xizhou Ancient Town to visit Yan Family Courtyard (Bai Nationality Three Tea Ceremony + Internet-famous rice fields). Go to Shangri-La to visit Tiger Leaping Cliff (including round- trip elevator) and visit Dukezong Ancient Town (Guishan Park and the world's largest prayer wheel).

Day 4: Shangri-La - Lijiang
Head to Songzanlin Temple (including eco-friendly car), then to Yila Grassland, Napahai (leisure coffee + viewing platform). Take a bus to Lijiang, then visit Lijiang Ancient Town + Sifang Street.

Day 5: Li Jiang
Visit the Jade Dragon Snow Mountain Scenic Area Glacier Park (including shuttle bus + cable car round trip) and visit Ganhaizi (Zhang Yimou-Impression Lijiang Live Song and Dance Show). After that, go to Baishui River + Blue Moon Valley, visit Yushui Village (Dongba blessing + learning to write Dongba characters + DIY hieroglyphic T-shirts).

Day 6: Chuxiong
Head to Fengyang Village (Windy Courtyard + Tie-Dyeing Room + Guanyin Well). Then visit Cang'er Liuxiang (Thousands of Mu of Rice Fields + Internet Celebrity Carriage + Travel Photography + Ecological Corridor). Finally, visit Chuxiong, Yi Ancient Town + Bonfire Party.

Day 7: Chuxiong - Kunming
Head to Shilin Scenic Area + including scenic area eco-friendly bus, Golden Horse and Jade Rooster Pavilion, and visit the popular night market - Nanqiang Market.

Day 8: Kunming - Penang
Visit Waterfall Park, Jinri Tower and Green Lake Park. Then go to the airport to take flight direct back to Penang .
