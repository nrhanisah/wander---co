KOREA

6 Days Korea Busan

From RM 4,588 PER PAX

Day 1: Penang - Transit
Departure flight from Penang International Airport to Busan.

Day 2: Transit - Busan
Gamcheon Village (Picturesque attraction featuring a village of colorful houses, painted murals, shops and cafes), Yongdusan Park (Nice park located at the base of the symbolic 120m high Busan Diamond Tower), Kukje Market (One of the largest markets) and Jagalchi Market (A traditional Busan market).

Day 3: Busan - Gyeongju - Busan
Bulguksa Temple (A UNESCO Heritage Site and is the essence of Korean Buddhist culture in the 8th century), Anapji Pond (A part of the palace complex of ancient Silla), Gyochon Hanok Village with Hanbok Wearing (A historic traditional hanok village) and Cheomseongdae Observatory ( A 7th-century CE observatory tower located in Gyeongju, the capital of the Silla Kingdom of ancient Korea).

Day 4: Busan
Haeundae BlueLine Park (Inc. Capsule Train), Yonggung Temple (A Buddhist temple complex is a large one and one of few in Korea to be set on the seaside), Songdo Ocean Cable Car, Yonggung Cloud Bridge & Skywalk and Activities - Skyline Luge Ride (2 times).

Day 5: Busan
BIFF Square, Gwangbokdong Street and Shopping Time - National Ginseng Shop, Cosmetic Shop, Hu Gan Bao, Duty Free Shop, Seomyeon Street.

Day 6: Busan - Transit - Penang
Transfer to airport for departure flight home.
